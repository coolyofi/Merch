Objective 
The Apple Retail customer experience requires accurate product placement and 
ﬂawless execution. This guide helps you correctly space, place, and cable manage 
demo products throughout the store. Use Plano to identify product placement, 
orientation, and order. 
Great visual merchandising blends analytics with intuition. Don’t rely only on math — 
understand the merchandising strategy ﬁrst. Check if the spacing looks right and 
aligns with the strategy. Reﬂect on the overall impact, review your work, and adjust as 
needed to achieve balance.
Before getting started, use these steps to achieve ﬂawless product merchandising. 
Slow down and focus on the details.
1.Use Plano to identify products, solutions of products, and necessary elements.
2.Use the spacing principles (provided in this guide) to determine product 
placement.
3.Use category-speciﬁc merchandising guidance (provided in this guide) to cable 
manage each device and complete merchandising.
4.Use guidance in Under Table Cable Management to align and tighten cables. 
5.Conﬁrm merchandising and use materials in Hello, such as APPLE Guides and 
other engagement guides, to share strategy changes with your team.

Solutions of Products 
A solution of products is deﬁned as a hero product merchandised with other hero 
products, accessories, or elements (including 2x3 product signage) as one complete 
solution. Solutions are identiﬁed via Plano, and examples are provided in the image

Spacing Principles 
General Spacing Principle 
Use the General Spacing Principle to identify the correct length of space between 
products on a wall, table, accessory ﬁxture, or Avenue counter when the product is 
plugged in under the table or inside of a wall. This principle applies to any product, 
regardless of product size or surface size, and should be adjusted according to table 
length (A). Ensure that products are never placed over the ﬂitch. (See “X÷2” in the 
image below to locate the ﬂitch on a table).

The only exception to the General Spacing Principle would be when tables are 
merchandised with 2x3 product signage. Read the ”2x3 Product Signage Spacing 
Guidance” section for various Product Zone tables.

A = Length of table, wall, or Avenue counter
B = Sum of the width of all solutions
C = Number of solutions

Counter Preparation
Cut a roll of Velcro in half lengthwise. Adhere a continuous strip of the rough side of 
the Velcro under the counter. Ensure the strips don’t obstruct the rolling mechanisms 
and aren’t visible when the counter is closed or visible in the reﬂection of the stainless 
steel. 
Where applicable, place an alarm box every ﬁve feet in the trough behind the counter. 
Place power strips as needed. These items don’t need to be adhered. 
Cut six-inch strips of soft Velcro. Remove the ﬁlm and fold the strip lengthwise so it 
adheres to itself and both sides of the strip are soft. Secure cables taught behind the 
counters, using this strip of soft Velcro. Excess cable should be coiled into individual 
circles and secured with two 4-inch natural zip ties.

For iPhone demos, place devices merchandised on third-party MagSafe chargers 
three inches apart.
For MacBook demo, place two inches from the edges of the counter, and place Mac 
accessories to the right of the demo with equal spacing. If accessories are shown on 
either side of the Mac in Planogram, center the Mac on the counter and place Mac 
accessories to the left and right with equal spacing.
Reference Plano for detailed demo product placement.
Audio Standards 
Demo AirPods Max, Beats headphones, and Beats Pill should only be connected to 
power overnight, after the store closes. Keep the charging cable tucked into the wall 
or table during the day for easy access.
AirPods Max
Demo AirPods Max must always be displayed with the Digital Crown on the right side 
when you approach. 
Beats Headphones
Demo Beats headphones must always be displayed with the ear cushion on the left 
side when you approach.
In instances where a Beats headphone is merchandised behind a 1-Device MagSafe 
Riser, run the cable underneath the Beats headphone stand. 
Beats Pill 
Remove the lanyard from both demo units when Beats Pill is merchandised in stereo 
pair. Maintain the lanyard when the demo Beats Pill is standalone. Secure the lanyard 
in a safe place Backstage when not in use.
